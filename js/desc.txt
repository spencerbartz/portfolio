Puzzle version 1.0 - Puzzle pieces can be "grabbed" by clicking on them and they stick to the cursor until you plop them down somewhere.
I got the idea from seeing a friend's "dress your character" web app he said was "DHTML." I became curious about this technique so I made this 
while I was living in Japan (hence the seafood theme!) using JavaScript to manipulate <div> positions based on mouse events.
